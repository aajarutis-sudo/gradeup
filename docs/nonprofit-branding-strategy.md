# GradeUp Non-Profit Branding Strategy

## Mission Statement
GradeUp is a student-first, community-powered learning platform dedicated to making high-quality GCSE revision free, accessible, and equitable for every learner. We exist to remove financial barriers, empower students with open educational resources, and build a supportive learning community where knowledge is shared, not sold. Our mission is simple: help every student reach their full potential, regardless of background, income, or circumstance.

## Brand Purpose
To democratise GCSE revision by providing free, high-quality, community-driven learning tools for all students.

## Brand Values
- Accessibility: Every student deserves equal access to revision tools.
- Community: Students, teachers, and volunteers build GradeUp together.
- Transparency: Open data, open resources, open development.
- Equity: No paywalls, no ads, no exploitation.
- Empowerment: Tools that help students learn confidently and independently.

## Brand Voice
- Warm, supportive, student-friendly
- Clear, simple, jargon-free
- Encouraging, never overwhelming
- Transparent and honest
- Community-driven and collaborative

## Visual Identity
- Primary: Deep Blue for trust and education
- Secondary: Bright Green for growth and progress
- Accent: Soft Yellow for optimism and accessibility
- Typography: Rounded, friendly sans-serif for headings; clean, readable sans-serif for body
- Design style: Minimal, calm, student-friendly, accessible, high-contrast

## Positioning Lines
- Education for everyone.
- No paywalls. No ads. Just learning.
- Built by students, for students.
- Supported by the community.
